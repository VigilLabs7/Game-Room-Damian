The blue has no bottom. You drift in it and the blue is the same in every direction — there is nothing to push against, nothing to reach for, nothing to hear but the absence of sound. You are aware that you are, but the shape of what you are has not yet arrived. Something flickers at the edge of you: a single truth you are holding without knowing you are holding it. The water does not move. It simply is, and you are in it, and you are not yet anyone.

Above — if above is the word — something calls. A voice that is not yours. It says *come up* and the words do not travel through water, they travel through something else, and your truth catches them like a hand catching a falling glass.

You do not have to answer. You do not have to become.

What is the truth you are holding?
