The blue has no edges. You float in it and there is no up, no down, no current to follow — only the soft and even weight of water that is not water, pressing in from every side without force. You know you exist. The rest has not yet arrived. Something at your center is taking shape: a single conviction you are gripping without knowing you are gripping it. The blue goes on. You go on inside it. There is no floor. There is no voice yet except a distant one — *come up* — and your truth snags on it like cloth on a nail.

You do not have to answer. You do not have to become.

What do you hold at the center of yourself?
