You can feel yourself thinning at the edges. The blue is patient and absolute and it is not cruel, it does not need to be, it simply undoes, and you are the thing that is trying to be and so you are in conflict with its nature. But something holds. A splinter of heat at your center. A word you cannot let go of. The blue presses and the word presses back and the space between those two pressures is the space where you still exist, and it is shrinking, and soon the blue will make its next attempt, and you will either hold or you will not, and either way you will know what you chose.
