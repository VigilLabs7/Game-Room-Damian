The blue has no floor. No surface. No horizon. Just the same shade in every direction — not dark, not light, something before both. You are here. You are aware of that much. The awareness is the only thing that is solid, and even it is thin, held together by something you cannot quite name.

Something is taking shape. Not a thought — something before thought. A single concept you are clinging to without knowing you are clinging to anything at all. It is the only thing that is yours. The only thing keeping you from dissolving entirely into the blue.

There is nothing else. No sound but the not-sound of being. No other presence, no voice, no edge — only the slow drift and the sense that something is about to break.

What do you hold?

You are here, in the black, and the black is not empty. It is *waiting*.
