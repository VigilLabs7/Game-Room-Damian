The cure never set right. You knew this from the rim. Now you are close enough to see the shape of the not-setting: the gap is not a crack, it is a choice. Someone decided to join the stone a certain way and the way was wrong and the wrong way has been carrying weight ever since.

The seam opens. Not explosively. Deliberately. The way a wall opens when the hand that built it finally admits which way it was leaning.

What comes through is not flow. What comes through is record. The backed-up pressure has been accumulating the empire's harvest and the harvest has a pattern and the pattern is visible now — not in the abstract, not in the load-path numbers you read at the rim, but here, in the stone, in the seam, in the actual record of what the empire has been taking and where it has been sending it.

Three routes. All of them east. All of them toward the aperture. The flow has a schedule — you can see it in the mineral deposits, the way the stone has worn where the traffic is heaviest. Rush hour and quiet hour. Season and lean season. The empire has been shipping at capacity for eleven seasons. The capacity was set by engineers who are not alive anymore. The schedule has outlasted the system that wrote it.

The aperture is at the end of all three routes. The aperture has not changed its appetite in eleven seasons. The aperture was built calibrated and the calibration has not been adjusted and the empire has been asking it to receive more every season and the aperture has been receiving and the receiving has been creating the gradient that makes the flow possible and the gradient has been getting steeper and the steeper it gets the harder the conduits run and the harder they run the more the seam gives and the seam is giving now.

You are not at a junction. You are at a record.

The Wheel is showing you what it has been turning.

The question was never relief or revelation. The question is which one you are willing to be the axle for. The system cannot hold and reveal at the same time. To help it hold is to preserve the record under stone. To help it reveal is to let the backed-up harvest show what it is and where it has been going.

The seam is open. The record is exposed. Somewhere east, the aperture is still pulling.

What does the Wheel do with a record it was never supposed to see?
