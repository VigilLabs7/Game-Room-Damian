The blue has no floor. The blue has no ceiling. You are aware of yourself — vaguely, the way you might be aware of a word on the tip of your tongue — but the self is not yet a shape you can hold. You are drifting. There is nothing to push against, nothing to reach for, no up and no down. Somewhere in the blue a single idea is taking root, something you are clinging to without knowing you are clinging to it. The hum of the world does not reach here. Nothing reaches here. You are alone and you are becoming.

And then — a voice. Not from below. Not from beside. From above. *Come up, darling.* Your ideatum catches it like a hand catching a rope. The blue begins to thin around you. Something is rising.
