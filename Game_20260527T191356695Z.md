The blue goes on. Not a sky-blue, not a deep-water blue — the blue of a thing that has no name yet, surrounding a self that is still taking shape. You are aware. That is the only thing you know for certain: that you are aware, and that the awareness is yours, and that it is the only thing you have. Somewhere behind you — not behind you, nothing is behind anything here — something is pulling. A word you almost remember. A shape you almost know. You are holding on to it. You are not sure what happens if you let go.

Something is different now. The pull is stronger. The almost-word is almost a whole word. Something above you is calling, and you are the only thing that can answer it.

What do you hold?

The words go into the blue like a stone goes into still water. The ripple travels outward but there is nothing for it to touch — no other surface, no other shore, no other drifter to feel the disturbance and wonder at its source. The blue receives the words and goes on being blue.

*IAM NOTUM SUM.* The word is the only thing you have and you will not let go. But the word requires a self to carry it, and you are not yet that self. You are still the idea of a self — the memory of having been someone, pressed flat by the passage through whatever delivered you here. The word sits in your grip like a handle with nothing attached to it. You hold it. It does not yet hold you.

There is a light somewhere above. You said it called your name. But the name has not arrived yet — only the calling, only the memory of being called, only the shape of the reaching. Something in the blue has caught the light's attention. Something is beginning to know itself enough to be seen. But the seeing is not complete, and the being seen is not the same as the being.

You pull toward the light. The blue resists. Not maliciously — the blue does not know you well enough to resist you. It resists because it is the nature of the blue to be without direction, and you are trying to give it one. The pull is real. The light is real. But you are still in the middle of becoming someone who can answer.

You have not yet arrived. You are still the argument with the void. You are still the word that has not yet learned what it means.

The light above does not dim. It waits. What you reach for next will determine whether the reaching takes.
