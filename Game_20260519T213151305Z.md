The blue is everything. You drift in it — if *drift* is even the word for a motion without water, without direction, without a body to move. There is no surface to break toward. No floor to sink past. Only the same submerged blue in every direction, and the faint, persistent sense that you are holding on to something. A concept. A weight. A single principle that refuses to dissolve even here.

You are not yet *you* in the way the world will make you *you*. But the idea of you is taking shape in the blue, and it is gripping the call.

Something moves above. A voice — not from beside you, not from below. From somewhere the blue cannot reach.

**Speak your name. Name what you carry. Declare what you are.**
