## Turn 1

**Player:** I am Tinder.

My ideatum is "Every fire was once a spark."

The blue shivers and I answer it the way I answer everything — with the ember I have been carrying since the night the mill burned. I am small and I am patient and I have never forgotten the arc. Ask me what I am holding onto and I will tell you: the direction the spark went. Downward. Into the heat that makes things burn. The rest is just waiting to find out what it lands on.

The ember answers before the blue does.

It has been carrying the mill fire since the night everything burned — the grain and the beams and the careful hands that tended both. The ember does not speak. It does not need to. It holds the direction: downward, into heat, into the place where things stop being what they were and start becoming what comes next. The spark fell and the spark landed and the spark found something to become. The ember is the memory of that falling. The ember is the direction the spark went.

The blue thins around the ember. Not everywhere — only around the thing you are holding onto. The rest of the blue is indifferent, vast, the same blue it has always been. But here, in the space around the ember, the blue is beginning to understand that it is not everything. Something else is here. Something that does not belong to the blue and has never belonged to it. Something that was fire before the blue had a word for fire.

You have hands. You are not certain when you acquired them — but you have them now, and they are closing around the ember, and the closing is the first thing you have done in this place that you chose to do. The blue did not give you the ember. The blue does not know what the ember is. The ember is yours because you carried it here from somewhere the blue cannot reach.

The blue is still pressing in. The blue is still everything. But the ember is burning and the direction is set and the spark has already fallen. The question is not whether the spark will land. The question is what it finds when it does.

What do you hold onto?
