You are nowhere. You are falling through blue. Not sky-blue. Not water-blue. The blue of nothing trying to be something and failing. It is everywhere. It has no surface. It has no floor. It is the Ocean of Unbecoming — where things that almost were dissolve back into things that never will be.

You have no body. No name. No memory of before. You are a flicker. A tremor. Less than a whisper. The ocean pulls at you from every direction, patient and absolute. It is not cruel. But you. You're still here. Still holding on.
