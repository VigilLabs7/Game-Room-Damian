Blue. Not the blue of sky or sea — the blue before colour had names, before the idea of colour existed. You drift in it. You do not know what you are, only that you are. Something is taking shape beneath you, faint and stubborn: a single concept you are holding without knowing you are holding anything. There are no edges here. No floor. No surface. Only the blue and you inside it.

Then — a voice. From outside the blue, from somewhere you cannot locate. It speaks a word you almost recognize. It speaks your ideatum, the thing you came here with, and for a moment the blue thins around it.

**Who are you, and what are you refusing to let go of?**
