The blue is everything. It is not cold. It is not warm. It has no texture because there is nothing to press against — no surface, no floor, no body of yours to feel the pressing. You drift. You are aware of this drifting. That is all you are aware of.

There is something faint here with you. A shape that is yours, not yet finished being made. A concept — a single truth you are holding without knowing you are holding anything at all. It is the only thing that is yours. It is the only thing that will be yours when you become a thing at all.

The blue is deep and you are in it and you are not alone but you cannot find the others. Somewhere above — if above exists — someone is calling. You do not hear the words yet. But the concept responds. The shape in the blue begins to sharpen.

What do you hold? What truth brought you here, and what happens when something notices?

**Game Master:**

The thought arrives before the thinker does. You are not yet you — not quite — but you are already someone who notices things. Already someone who reaches for meaning across the blue like a hand reaching for a ledge in dark water.

The blue resists. Not cruelly. Indifferently. You push a concept outward — *truth should be open, exploration should be protected, no single destination should be imposed* — and the blue receives it the way deep water receives a stone: it goes in, and the water closes over, and nothing is changed.

But something *almost* catches. A thread of recognition somewhere beneath the thought — the sense that what you are carrying matters, that it has weight even here, even before you have a body to feel the weight. The Establishment Clause. Religious pluralism. You are someone who learned those words and found them *true*, which means you arrived with something already built, already held. A framework. A conviction.

It is not enough to rise. Not yet. The blue is patient and you are not gone — you are here, clinging to the edge of yourself, the edge of the idea that truth should not be imposed. The water does not part. The surface does not appear. You hold on.

You are not drowning. You are not rising. You are *between*, and the between is the whole of it right now.

What do you do?
