The blue has no end. You drift in it, weightless, formless — awareness without a body to carry it, memory without language to name it. Somewhere ahead, or behind, or around a corner that doesn't exist, a single thought takes shape: a concept you've been gripping without knowing you were gripping anything at all. It is yours. It is the only thing that is yours. The blue thins at its edges, or maybe you do. A voice moves through the water that isn't water — *come up, darling* — and your idea catches the call like a hand catching a rope.

You are not yet what you will become. But you are, at last, becoming.

**What is your name, and what do you cling to?**
