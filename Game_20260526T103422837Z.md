# The Ocean of Unbecoming

The only thing that is certain is the concept — the one thing you are holding onto without knowing you are holding onto anything at all.

You are alone here. You have always been alone here.

There is no surface. There is no floor. There is no horizon. Only the same blue in every direction, and somewhere far above you — so far it might not exist — a voice you almost remember. Something about rising. Something about being called.

Your fingers feel like they are gripping something, though you cannot feel what it is.

**What do you do?**
