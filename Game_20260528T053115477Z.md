You are alone here. Every drifter is alone here — even when others are technically near, they cannot be found. The hum of the world does not reach this deep. Nothing reaches. You are the only thing in your reach.

A voice could call from above. Something could catch the shape you are becoming. Or the blue could simply thin until the shape inside it is the only shape there is, and you rise without being called.
