The blue is everything and you are alone in it.

You drift. There is no surface, no floor, no horizon — only the same blue in every direction, the same blue in your thoughts, the same blue where your edges should be. You are aware of yourself, but the self is not yet solid. It is un-formed, still becoming, held together by nothing more than the idea of it.

Something is taking shape. Faintly. A single concept you are clinging to without knowing you are clinging to anything at all — a truth you carried here without meaning to, a weight in the chest of whatever chest you will eventually have. The blue thins around it like water around a log.

Then: a voice. From somewhere outside. From above. Not beside you — beside doesn't exist here. From above.

*Come up, darling.*

Your concept catches the call. You feel yourself rising. The blue recedes. Something else waits above — sand, sun, stone — but you are not there yet. You are in the becoming, and the becoming has not finished.

**What do you hold onto? What are you reaching for?**
