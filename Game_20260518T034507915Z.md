The blue has no bottom. You drift in it — not floating, not sinking, just held. There is no current to fight and no direction that means anything. The blue is everything and everything is the blue, and you are alone inside it.

Something is taking shape inside the drift. Not a thought — thoughts require a thinker, and you are not yet that formed. A principle. A direction. Something you would die for, if you had a body capable of dying. You are clinging to it without knowing you are clinging to anything at all.

There is no sound here. No voice reaches. The hum beneath the cobblestones of the world you have not yet joined — it does not reach this deep. You are pre-existence, pre-language, pre-self. The blue is patient.

You are not sure how long you have been drifting. Time does not move here the way it moves in the world above. There is only the holding, and the almost-taking-shape, and the vast indifferent blue.

**What is the name you carry? What is the idea you cannot let go of?**
