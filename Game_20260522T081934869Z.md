The blue goes all the way down and all the way up and there is no surface. You drift in it without drifting — there is no water moving you, no current, no up or down. The blue is everywhere and you are mostly the blue, except for the small place where you are holding on.

You are not alone in the blue. You cannot see them. You cannot reach them. But somewhere in the blue another drifter is holding on to a different word, and you will never know their name.

The hum does not reach here. Nothing from the world reaches here. You are pre-existence, and the threshold is waiting.

You have a name. Say it. You have a reason. Give it. What brought you here will not let you stay.
