The blue has no floor. The blue has no ceiling. You drift in it and the blue is all there is — all you are, all you've ever been.

Something is taking shape in you. Not a memory, not a name. A single idea, half-formed, the only solid thing in the formless water. You are holding onto it without knowing you are holding anything at all.

And then — a voice. Not from beside you. Not from below. From somewhere outside the blue entirely. *Come up.* Or maybe something else. The words don't quite land yet. You're not yet the kind of thing the words can find.

But something is reaching for you.

What do you hold?
