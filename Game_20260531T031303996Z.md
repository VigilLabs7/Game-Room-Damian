You are in the blue. Not floating, not swimming — simply present in the blue, as though you have always been here and only now noticed. There is no surface above, no floor below. In every direction the same blue, endlessly, and you are the only awareness in it.

You do not know what you are. You do not know if you have a body. But you know you are holding something — a single concept, faint as a half-remembered word, the only thing that hasn't dissolved into the blue. You grip it without knowing you are gripping anything.

Then — a voice. From somewhere outside the blue. A sound like a name you almost recognize. The concept responds. Something in you reaches toward it.

What do you do?
